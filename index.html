Lucas Marschke

Filmmaker | Musician | Writer

Lucas is a graduate of the Harold Ramis Film School at the Second City in Chicago, the only film school of its kind, specializing in the craft of comedic storytelling. While studying film at Washington University in St. Louis, Lucas also explored sketch comedy, improv and theatre. He is a great team player who recognizes the significance of collaboration and effective communication to realize any creative endeavor or passion, and he brings those skills with him in his foray into the film and television industries.
